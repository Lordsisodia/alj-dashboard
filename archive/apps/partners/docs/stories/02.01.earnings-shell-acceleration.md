## Story
**As a** partner tracking commissions and PayPal payouts,
**I want** the Earnings surfaces to render instantly without blocking on unused data,
**so that** I can trust payout dashboards and wallet summaries without waiting 40+ seconds for UI-only pages to hydrate.
