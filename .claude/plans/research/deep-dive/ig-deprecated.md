They're explicitly NOT designed for analytics, research, or any kind of data aggregation.\n\n## Why Meta Keeps Deprecating APIs\n\nUnderstanding Meta's motivation helps you plan:\n\n1. **Cambridge Analytica aftermath:** After 2018, Meta locked down data access aggressively\n2. **Revenue protection:** They want you to buy ads, not analyze organic performance\n3. **Competitive moat:** Limiting data access prevents competitors from building on their platform\n4. **User privacy:** Real concern, though often used as justification for business decisions\n\n**The pattern:** Every year, expect more restrictions.
