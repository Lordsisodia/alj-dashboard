## What plan types does Airtable offer?

Airtable offers two distinct types of plans: Self-serve and sales lead plans.

## What is a self-serve plan?

> Note
>
> - While Business plans can be purchased independently, we also offer a more personalized Business plan through our sales team. [Contact them](https://www.airtable.com/contact-sales) to find out if a sales-managed plan might better support your team's needs.
>
> - Business and Enterprise scale plans require private email domains, meaning domains like Gmail, Yahoo, etc, are ineligible when upgrading to either plan.

Self-serve plans are plans that include monthly and annual billing options—allowing you to manage billing directly through Airtable. Self-serve plans include:

- [Team plans](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-plans#team-plan)

- [Business plans](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-plans#business-plans-selfserve)

- [Non-profit plans](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-plans#nonprofit-plans)

- [Student plans](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-plans#student-plans)

- [Education plans](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-plans#education-plans)


## What is a sales-lead plan?

> Note
>
> - Airtable's Sales team sells and manages the [Business](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-plans#business-plans) and [Enterprise Scale plans](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-plans#enterprise-scale-plans). [Contact them](https://www.airtable.com/contact-sales) to find out if either plan might better support your team's needs.
>
> - Business and Enterprise scale plans require private email domains, meaning domains like Gmail, Yahoo, etc, are ineligible when upgrading to either plan.

Sales-led plans are built in collaboration with our sales team. The sales-led plans include:

- Sales-led Business plans

- Enterprise Scale plans


## How are self-serve plans billed?

- Self-serve plans include monthly and annual billing options and are billed per workspace, not per account.

- Self-serve plans allow customers to [upgrade individual workspaces](https://support.airtable.com/docs/changing-your-airtable-workspace-plan#changing-your-workspace) while simultaneously having workspaces on the Free plan.

  - Team plan owners are billed for any [workspace](https://support.airtable.com/docs/adding-a-workspace-collaborator) or [base collaborators](https://support.airtable.com/docs/adding-a-base-collaborator) with Commenter or higher-level permissions.

  - Business plan are billed for any [workspace](https://support.airtable.com/docs/adding-a-workspace-collaborator) or [base collaborators](https://support.airtable.com/docs/adding-a-base-collaborator) with Editor or higher-level permissions.
- Team, Business, and Enterprise Scale accounts include read-only collaborators, form submissions, and [share links](https://support.airtable.com/docs/creating-a-base-share-link-or-a-view-share-link).


## How are sales-led plans billed?

Billing for Enterprise Scale and Business plans is calculated differently. [Reach out to our sales team](https://www.airtable.com/contact-sales) for additional context and information.

## Free plan

> Note
>
> After creating a new account in Airtable, your first workspace is automatically upgraded to a 14-day complimentary trial of the [Team plan](https://support.airtable.com/docs/new-airtable-plans-overvier-content-delete-once-done#team-plans). After your 14-day Team trial is over, you can choose to remain on the Free plan or [upgrade your plan](https://support.airtable.com/docs/changing-your-airtable-workspace-plan#changing-your-workspace%E2%80%99s-plan). Future Free plan workspaces created under the same account will not receive this complimentary Team trial.

The Free plan is intended for individuals or very small teams just getting started with Airtable.

## Student plans

> Note
>
> Students and educators with a ".edu" email address are granted a 120-day Team plan trial immediately after creating a workspace on the Free plan. This trial starts before being approved for a discounted or complimentary plan.

Airtable offers students free Team accounts for a single workspace for 6-24 months, depending on your application approval and expected graduation date.

Check out our [Discounted Airtable plans article](https://support.airtable.com/docs/nonprofit-and-educational-plans-faqs#applying-for-discounted-airtable-plans) to apply for a free Student plan.

> Note
>
> The Student plan does not include the following features:
>
> - [Run a script](https://support.airtable.com/docs/run-a-script-action) automation action
>
> - Unlimited records per base (limited to 50,000)
>
> - Unlimited attachment storage (limited to 20GB per base)
>
> - Some advanced customization options for forms and views

What features are not included with Team trial plans?

The Team trial plan does not include the following features otherwise associated with the Team plan:

- Workflows Custom Script Action - Custom JavaScript/Python scripts in workflow automations

  - [Run a script](https://support.airtable.com/docs/run-a-script-action) automation action
- Premium Forms - Advanced form features (beyond basic forms)

- Unlimited records per base (limited to 50,000)

- Unlimited attachment storage (limited to 20GB per base)

- 10000 AI credits per user, per month (limited to 7500 AI credits per user above read-only permissions per trial)

- Some advanced customization options for forms and views


What features are limited when using Team trial plans?

The Team trial plan limits the following features:

- Records per base: 50,000

- Attachment space: 10GB per base

- Revision history: 365 days

- Automation runs: 25,000 per month

- Synced tables: 10 per application


## FAQs

How are self-serve plans different from Business and Enterprise plans sold through Airtable’s Sales team?

- Self-serve plans are plans that include monthly and annual billing options. These plans allow you to manage your billing directly through Airtable.

- Sales team managed plans are personalized through our sales team and require private email domains, meaning domains like Gmail, Yahoo, etc, are ineligible when upgrading.


Why am I receiving an error when attempting to sign up for a Business or Enterprise Scale plan?

If you received the error, “The email address associated with your account is not eligible to be upgraded to the Business plan. Please ensure you use a valid email domain.” it’s because you’re attempting to sign up using a public email domain like Gmail, Yahoo, etc.

Business and Enterprise Scale plans require nonpublic/private email domains—a domain that's exclusive to a business or organization.

What are private email domains?

Private email domains are domains that are exclusive to a business or organization, unlike Gmail, Yahoo, etc.

Does my plan’s record limit include records hosted in other tables?

Yes, your plan’s record limit is cumulative over different tables. For example, a base with one table that has 50,000 records and a base with two tables of 25,000 records each would both be at the limits of the Team plan.

How many bases can a single workspace store?

1500 Bases per workspace

How many tables, views, and fields can a single base store?

- 1000 Tables per base

- 1000 Views per base

- 500 Fields per table


How do I confirm the remaining days of my Team trial plan?

To confirm how many days of your Team trial plan remains:

1. Open your [Airtable home screen](https://airtable.com/).

How do I confirm when my monthly or annual workspace plan renews?

To confirm when your monthly or annual workspace plan renews:

1. Open your [Airtable account overview](https://airtable.com/account).

2. Select the workspace with the plan you want to review from the "Workspace settings" section.

3. Click **Billing**.

1. Your renewal date is visible under the “Billing history” column.

Can I transfer ownership of a workspace on the Free plan to another collaborator?

No. On the Free plan, changing the workspace Owner isn’t supported—upgrading to a paid plan is required to transfer ownership to another collaborator.
