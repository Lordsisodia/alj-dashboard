### 1.1 Descript

**Category:** Text-based video/audio editing + AI post-production

**Core AI Features:**
- **Text-based editing:** Edit video by editing its transcript — delete text, delete video. Estimated 60-70% reduction in editing time for spoken-word content.
- **Underlord AI co-editor:** 30+ AI tools bundled into a single co-editor interface.
- **Auto Clip Detection / Highlights:** AI identifies key moments from long-form content for clip extraction.
- **Auto Captions:** One-click animated captions with customizable fonts, colors, word highlighting, positioning. Transcription in 25 languages.
- **Filler Word Removal:** Automatically detects and removes "um," "ah," "like," repeated words from audio/video.
- **Studio Sound:** Single-click audio enhancement — noise removal, speech clarity, studio-quality output.
- **Eye Contact Correction:** AI adjusts gaze to appear camera-direct while reading scripts.
- **Green Screen / Background Removal:** No physical green screen needed.
- **Overdub / AI Voice Cloning:** Fix mistakes by typing; AI regenerates in speaker's cloned voice.
- **AI Tagging/Categorization:** Metadata and content indexing via transcript content.
- **Caption/Subtitle Generation:** Animated captions, 25 languages, customizable templates.

**AI Brief Generation:** Not native — Underlord can summarize content but not generate production briefs.

### 1.4 Opus Clip

**Category:** AI video repurposing — long-form to short-form social clips

**Core AI Features:**
- **Auto Clip Detection:** Scans full source video, outputs clips with captions, vertical reframing, and Virality Score. Industry-leading for this specific use case.
- **Virality Score (0-99):** Scores each clip on hook strength, emotional flow, perceived value, and trend alignment. Practical sorting heuristic — not a guarantee.
- **Active Speaker Detection:** Auto-identifies speaker, keeps them centered in 9:16 vertical frame — works in multi-person interviews.
- **Auto Captions:** ~95% accuracy on clean audio (claims 97-99%), 25+ languages. Includes 10+ animated caption templates, keyword highlighting, auto emoji insertion, speaker color coding, custom font upload (Pro).
- **AI Hook Generation:** Rewrites clip opening for maximum retention (Pro only).
- **AI B-Roll Insertion:** Auto-inserts relevant B-roll footage (Pro only).
- **Social Scheduling & Publishing:** Direct posting to YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X (Pro only).
- **AI Tagging:** Clips tagged with topic/subject classification.
- **API Access:** Business plan only — enables CMS/workflow automation integration.

**Content Performance Prediction:** Virality Score is the closest analog — predictive, not definitive.

**AI Brief Generation:** Not available.

**Trend Analysis:** No native trend intelligence — pairs well with TikTok Creative Center externally.

**On-Screen Text Extraction:** Captions generated from audio, not OCR from video frames.

### 1.6 Google Gemini (Video Analysis API)

**Category:** Foundation model with native multimodal video understanding

**Core Video Analysis Capabilities:**
- **Frame-by-Frame Processing:** Default 1 FPS sampling rate; configurable higher/lower for fast-action or long static videos.
- **On-Screen Text Extraction (OCR):** Extracts visible text from video frames — including conference captions, badges, uniforms, vehicles, backpacks, on-screen graphics.
- **Audio + Visual Synthesis:** Processes both audio and visual streams simultaneously for richer understanding.
- **Scene Segmentation:** Identifies and segments distinct scenes with timestamps.
- **Structured Data Extraction:** Outputs structured metadata from unstructured video content.
- **Speaker Identification:** Multi-pass approach — audio-only transcription first, then video frame speaker ID, then reinject for consolidation.
- **Multimodal Transcription:** Accurate transcription with speaker diarization and language support.
- **Moment Retrieval:** Identifies specific moments within videos using audio-visual cues. Example: finds 16 distinct product presentation segments in a 10-min keynote.
- **Entity Recognition:** Person names, company names, brand logos, places — from video frames.
- **Q&A over Video:** Answer arbitrary questions about video content.
- **Auto Tagging/Categorization:** Generate structured labels and metadata from video content.
- **Summary Generation:** Scene-by-scene and overall video summaries.

**Context Window & Video Length Support:**
- 1M token context: up to 1 hour at default resolution, 3 hours at low resolution.
- 2M token context (Gemini 2.5 Pro): processes full 1h41m conference keynotes.

**Model Versions (2026):**
- **Gemini 2.5 Pro:** State-of-the-art video understanding benchmarks, surpasses GPT-4.1 under comparable testing. Best for complex analysis tasks.
- **Gemini 2.5 Flash:** Faster, cost-efficient variant for high-throughput pipelines.
- **Gemini 3:** Latest generation — seamlessly synthesizes text, images, video, audio, code. Accurately transcribes 3-hour multilingual meetings with superior speaker ID. Extracts structured data from poor-quality document photos with 50%+ improvement over baseline models.

**API Integration Notes:**
- Configurable frame rate: `< 1 FPS` for long static videos, `> 1 FPS` for high-motion.
- File API stores video at 1 FPS + audio at 1Kbps single-channel with per-second timestamps.
- YouTube video analysis supported (see GoogleCloudPlatform/generative-ai repo).

**Pricing:** Pay-per-token via Google AI Studio / Vertex AI. Gemini 2.5 Flash is significantly cheaper than Pro; exact rates vary by tier and volume.

**Relevance to Editor Dashboard:**
This is the core intelligence engine for the planned dashboard. Gemini enables: auto-tagging content by topic/mood/action, extracting on-screen text from raw footage, generating scene summaries, identifying best clip moments, and briefing editors before they open CapCut.

## 3. CapCut Editing Tips for Editor Dashboard Integration

CapCut is the recommended editing endpoint for agency models/editors. Key workflow integrations:

### Core Features to Surface in Dashboard

1. **AutoCut:** Detects highlights, generates short-form clips, adds auto-subtitles, suggests templates. Configurable min/max clip length. Long-to-short conversion built-in.

2. **Auto Captions:** One-click speech-to-text in 130+ languages. Caption tab → select template → auto-sync with voiceover. 10+ animated styles. No manual sync needed.

3. **Long Video to Shorts:** Transform raw footage → trending vertical Reels/Shorts, with beat-synced cuts. Best paired with Gemini pre-analysis to identify the strongest 60-90 second window first.

4. **Transcript-Based Editing:** Edit by deleting transcript text — same paradigm as Descript but within CapCut's broader editing environment.

5. **Background Removal:** AI chroma key without physical green screen — one click.

6. **Auto Enhancer:** Automatically improves lighting, sound, color balance.

7. **AI Video Studio:** Conversational interface — build complete video from text prompt including style, avatar, structure.

8. **AI Avatars + Voiceovers:** Faceless video creation pipeline for models who prefer not to appear on camera.

### Recommended Editor Workflow (Gemini + CapCut)

```
1. Upload raw footage → Gemini 2.5 Pro analysis
   → Scene timestamps
   → On-screen text extraction
   → Topic/mood tags
   → AI-generated brief (hook suggestion, caption, format)
   → Best clip window identified

2. Brief surfaces in Dashboard editor card
   → Editor sees: clip window, suggested hook, caption draft, tags

3. Editor opens CapCut with pre-identified clip window
   → Use AutoCut on that segment
   → Apply auto-captions (verify against Gemini transcript)
   → Refine hook manually if needed
   → Export 9:16 for TikTok/Reels

4. Cross-reference TikTok Creative Center
   → Validate hook against trending hashtags/sounds
   → Update caption with trending terms
```

**CapCut Pricing:**
- Free tier: most core tools, auto-captions, basic AI, 1080p export, no watermark.
- Pro: $7.99–$19.99/mo (region-dependent). Unlocks 4K, advanced AI, premium effects.
- Cross-platform: desktop, mobile, browser with cloud sync.

## 4. Feature Gaps — Editor Dashboard (Gemini + CapCut Focus)

These are features present in competing tools that are NOT covered by a pure Gemini + CapCut stack, and would need to be built or integrated separately.

### Gap 1: Virality Scoring
**Missing from:** Gemini API, CapCut
**Available in:** Opus Clip (Virality Score 0-99)
**Gap impact:** Editors have no AI-driven signal for which clips are most likely to perform.
**Recommendation:** Integrate Opus Clip as a post-analysis step, OR build a custom scoring model using TikTok Creative Center trend data + Gemini content analysis as proxy signals. Alternatively, embed Opus Clip's API (Business plan required) into the dashboard pipeline.

### Gap 2: Real-Time Trend Intelligence
**Missing from:** Gemini API, CapCut, Descript, Pictory
**Available in:** TikTok Creative Center (free)
**Gap impact:** Editors create content without knowing whether the hook/sound/hashtag is currently trending.
**Recommendation:** Embed TikTok Creative Center Trend Discovery and Keyword Insights directly into the editor brief card. Free, no API key required for basic access.

### Gap 3: Content Performance Prediction (Pre-Publish)
**Missing from:** Gemini API, CapCut
**Available in:** TikTok Smart+ (ad-focused), Opus Clip (Virality Score)
**Gap impact:** No pre-publish quality gate — editors submit without a predicted outcome signal.
**Recommendation:** Use Opus Clip Virality Score as the pre-publish gate. Flag clips scoring below 60 for manual review before scheduling.

### Gap 4: AI Brief Generation (Structured, Actionable)
**Missing from:** CapCut (no brief output), Opus Clip, Pictory
**Available natively via:** Gemini API (custom prompt), TikTok Symphony
**Gap impact:** Editors receive raw footage with no structured direction.
**Recommendation:** This is Gemini's strongest contribution to the dashboard. A prompt template using Gemini 2.5 Pro on every upload generates: topic, key moments with timestamps, suggested hook, 3 caption variants, recommended hashtags, format (9:16 vs 16:9), and estimated watch-time.

### Gap 5: Active Speaker Tracking / Multi-Person Reframing
**Missing from:** Gemini API (identifies speakers but doesn't auto-reframe video), CapCut (manual)
**Available in:** Opus Clip (Active Speaker Detection + auto 9:16 reframe)
**Gap impact:** Model/talent content with 2+ people requires manual reframing in CapCut — time-consuming.
**Recommendation:** Route all multi-person content through Opus Clip for reframing before bringing into CapCut for caption/polish work.

### Gap 6: Custom Taxonomy Video Tagging
**Missing from:** Gemini API (prompt-flexible but no persistent taxonomy), CapCut
**Available in:** Mixpeek, TwelveLabs, Clarifai
**Gap impact:** Video library grows without structured, searchable tags aligned to agency categories (model name, campaign, platform, content type).
**Recommendation:** Use Gemini API with a strict JSON prompt schema to output tags in agency taxonomy format. Store in Convex DB per video record. This closes the gap without a third-party tagging API.

### Gap 7: Audio-Only Quality Enhancement
**Missing from:** Gemini API (analysis only, no enhancement)
**Available in:** Descript (Studio Sound), CapCut (Auto Enhancer — combined audio+video)
**Gap impact:** Raw model footage often has poor audio — needs enhancement before Gemini transcription for best accuracy.
**Recommendation:** Run CapCut Auto Enhancer as pre-processing step before Gemini upload. Or use Descript Studio Sound as a dedicated audio pass. CapCut is preferred since editors already work in it.

### Gap 8: Long-Form Repurposing at Scale (Batch)
**Missing from:** Gemini API (single-video focus), CapCut (manual per-video)
**Available in:** Pictory (batch, API), Opus Clip (batch processing)
**Gap impact:** High-volume content (e.g., 20 model shoots per week) cannot be auto-processed at scale.
**Recommendation:** For batch workloads, use Opus Clip Pro ($29/mo) as the batch processing layer. Gemini handles deep analysis on selected top clips post-OpusClip output. CapCut handles final polish and branding.

### Gap 9: On-Screen Text Extraction Workflow
**Missing from:** CapCut (no OCR)
**Available natively:** Gemini API (strong OCR), Google Cloud Video Intelligence API ($0.15/min)
**Gap impact:** Content with on-screen graphics, overlays, or text-heavy frames cannot be automatically indexed or searched.
**Recommendation:** Gemini 2.5 Pro handles this natively at no additional per-feature cost (token-based pricing). Include OCR extraction as part of standard Gemini analysis prompt on every upload.

### Gap 10: Search-and-Suggest Idea Discovery Within Own Library
**Missing from:** All tools listed — none offer search across the agency's own content library by semantic meaning.
**Partial coverage:** TikTok Creative Center (external trends only), Gemini API (can be used for this)
**Gap impact:** Editors cannot search "show me all clips where a model is unboxing" or "find all outdoor shoots with good lighting."
**Recommendation:** Build a semantic video search layer using Gemini embeddings over the agency's Convex-stored video metadata + frame tags. This is a Phase 2 feature but would be a significant differentiator.
