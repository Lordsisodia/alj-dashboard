# Help Center Content Plan – Client Pipeline

Starter articles (3 questions each) aligned to key pages.

## Pages / Articles

### Pipeline Dashboard
1) What do the pipeline summary metrics mean (prospects, active deals, velocity)?  
2) How often are pipeline numbers updated?  
3) How do I filter by stage or owner?

### Submit Client
1) What information is required to submit a new client?  
2) How is submission reviewed/approved and how long does it take?  
3) How do I track a submitted client’s status?

### Prospects
1) How are prospects prioritized or scored?  
2) How do tags (industry, size, urgency) affect views?  
3) How do I assign or reassign a prospect?

### Active Deals
1) What stages exist and what actions move a deal forward?  
2) How do I log meetings/notes and attach assets?  
3) How do I mark a deal as blocked and escalate?

### App Plan Generator
1) What inputs are required to generate an app plan?  
2) Can I edit/regenerate a plan after it’s created?  
3) How are generated plans shared with the client or team?
