# Help Center Content Plan – Notifications

Starter articles (3 questions each) for Notifications.

## Pages / Articles

### Notification Settings
1) Which events can I subscribe to (announcements, @mentions, wins, reactions, DMs)?  
2) How do I choose delivery channels (in-app, email, push)?  
3) How do quiet hours or do-not-disturb work?

### Channel Preferences
1) How do I mute a specific Community channel or conversation?  
2) How do I pin/highlight certain channels for priority alerts?  
3) How do per-channel settings interact with global notification settings?

### Digest & Summaries
1) What goes into a daily/weekly digest and can I customize it?  
2) How do I pause or change digest frequency?  
3) How are unread counts affected by digest delivery?

### Delivery & Reliability
1) What should I check if I’m not receiving notifications?  
2) How are bounces/failed deliveries handled?  
3) How do I report a false or duplicate alert?
